## Design Context

### Users

This product is used by foreign trade operators, purchasing staff, document specialists, warehouse staff, QC staff, finance staff, managers, and administrators. They use it throughout the workday to process contracts, samples, purchasing follow-up, QC, warehouse movements, documents, receivables/payables, and management reports.

### Brand Personality

Practical, trustworthy, precise. The interface should help users feel oriented and in control of cross-department trade workflows.

### Aesthetic Direction

Operational ERP workspace: light, dense, quiet, and scan-friendly. Avoid marketing composition, decorative hero areas, oversized cards, and single-hue purple/blue themes. Use restrained color to signal status, urgency, and ownership.

### Design Principles

- Keep primary workflows visible and quick to scan.
- Prefer clear tables, compact summaries, and predictable navigation over decorative layouts.
- Use status color sparingly for risk, overdue work, approvals, and completion.
- Make every page feel like a working surface for repeated daily use.
- Preserve readability and text fit across desktop and mobile widths.
